Samosa

A samosa is a fried or baked pastry with a savory filling, including ingredients such as spiced potatoes, onions, and peas. It may take different forms, including triangular, cone, or half-moon shapes, depending on the region.

Tikki

Aloo tikki is a golden fried-potato patty that is often stuffed with peas or dal and served with a variety of spicy chutneys and sometimes chickpeas, while aloo chaat is simply boiled potatoes that are cubed, fried, seasoned, and served hot.

Dosa

Dosas (Sada Dosa, Masala Dosa etc etc. ) are a typical part of Southern Indian or South Indian Cuisine, but the dish is now popular all over the Indian subcontinent. Dosa are made up of fermented rice and urad dal batter.
